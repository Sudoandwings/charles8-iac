About Me

Episode IV

A New Hope

In the bustling city of Accra, Ghana, a young traveler was born, his journey beginning in the warmth of a culture rooted in tradition and resilience. From his earliest steps, the boy's world was one of curiosity and wonder, driven by a spark that only grew with time.

The winds of change carried him across the ocean to the towering skyline of New York. Here, amid the relentless pace and endless energy, he was immersed in lessons that shaped his character and tested his resolve. It was here that he learned the value of ambition, the strength in humility, and the power of dreams.

Yet, the hand of fate was not finished guiding him. Through trials that tested his spirit, in 2008 he was led to Kansas City, a place where his journey would take root anew. Surrounded by a passionate, hopeful community—and a very below average NFL team that taught him the value of persistence, —the young traveler found a place to call home.

In this new chapter, opportunities blossomed. What he once viewed as distant dreams became stepping stones in a grand design. Every choice, every lesson, every blessing, all forged a path toward a single goal: to express his creativity and a growing love for technology. The journey was only beginning, and the young traveler now faced a future ready to be crafted by his own hand.
